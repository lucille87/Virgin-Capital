How can I invest with Virgin Capital?

To make an investment, you must first become a registered client of Virgin Capital. Once you are signed up, you can make your first deposit. It is important to note that all deposits must be made through the Members Area. You can login using your chosen username and password after you must have registered an account with Us.

We have made account creation as seamless as possible. Follow this link, fill in the registration form and then click on "Register".

What are the payment methods available?
At the moment, payments can be done via Credit/Debit Cards, Paypal, Skrill, Neteller, and Cryptocrrency(Bitcoin & Ethereum). Cryptocurrency is the easiest way to make payments online. While checking out your order, be sure to fill in the correct details for fast & hassle-free payment processing.
How long does it take for my deposit to reflect on my dashboard?
All depodits are processed within 12 hours Max. Payments via Crytocurrency(Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc) are processed within 2hrs; payments via Paypal and Skrill are processed within 4hours; payments via Credit/Debit cards are processed instantly; payments via Bank Wire or Direct Deposit will between 3-5 buisness days for processing.
How can I withdraw funds?
Login onto the your account using your username and password, navigate to the members area, and check the Withdraw section. Make sure to select the withdrawal option that is most convenient for you before placing your withdrawal.

Funds are usually available 6hours after placing withdrawal requests.

Who manages the funds?
Our Clients funds are managed by a team of specialized experts. We have highly trained personnels with extensive experience in every market or industry we invest in. Our team is one of the best in the industry as they operate with every professional nuance there is; we have also maintained a record minimal losses, with maximal rewards.
Can I make an additional deposit into my account when I still have an ongoing investment?
Yes, you can but all transactions are handled separately.
Can I lose money?
There is a risk involved with investing in all high yield investment programs. However, there are a few simple ways that can help you to reduce the risk of losing more than you can afford to. First, align your investments with your financial goals, in other words, keep the money you may need for the short-term out of more aggressive investments, reserving those investment funds for the money you intend to raise over the long-term. It's very important for you to know that we are real traders and that we invest members' funds on major investments. It is also important to note that we cover 90% of the losses.
